In the current age we live in where AI and search engine models are becoming more and more prevelent, it has become a priority I think to keep in tact human bonds and connections. One of the most important being with animals. In "Models All the Way Down" the author talks about the LAION-5B model, and how it cannot truly understand the world through the perspective of humans, as it is not one. This means the human-dog bod is outside of its understanding.

When someone looks at this image, I hope what they see of it is a tender image. Yet, when an AI looks at this image, all it sees is just data. In "Big? Smart? Clean? Messy? Data in the Humanities" the author points out that data is not really what is represented in an image. Instead it is just a partial representation of it. Because of that, it can never truly comprehend the tenderness of this image showcasing the human-dog bond.

Ultimatley, what I want people to take away from this is the importance of why we are so connected to dogs. Some of my reasons ultimatley come down to preferance, and that's okay! I of course love all dogs, but the ones I create true attachments with are a lot of the ones listed above. Overall, I see this piece as a celebration of the human and dog bond and how important it is to hold onto that.
